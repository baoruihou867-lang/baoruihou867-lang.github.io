Dictionary

工程英语词汇搜索

tolerance / 公差

The acceptable variation from a specified dimension or value.

Example: The shaft diameter is within tolerance.

drawing quality dimension

commissioning / 调试 / 投运

The process of testing and preparing equipment or a system for operation.

Example: Commissioning will start after installation is complete.

equipment site project

nonconformance / 不符合项

A failure to meet a requirement, specification, or standard.

Example: Please issue a nonconformance report for this batch.

quality inspection NCR

lead time / 交期 / 前置时间

The time required from order, request, or approval to delivery.

Example: The supplier confirmed a lead time of four weeks.

supply chain schedule

change request / 变更申请

A formal request to change scope, design, cost, schedule, or requirements.

Example: The design update must be submitted as a change request.

project scope cost

acceptance criteria / 验收标准

The conditions that must be met before work, parts, or systems can be accepted.

Example: Please confirm the acceptance criteria before production.

quality contract inspection

Workplace Patterns

常见工程沟通表达

确认需求

Could you confirm the required tolerance and surface finish?

请确认要求的公差和表面处理。

说明问题

We found a deviation during incoming inspection.

我们在来料检验中发现了偏差。

推动进度

Please share the updated schedule and the next action owner.

请提供更新后的计划以及下一步负责人。

提出变更

This change may affect cost, lead time, and validation scope.

此变更可能影响成本、交期和验证范围。
